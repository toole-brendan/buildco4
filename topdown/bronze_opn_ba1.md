**Availability Type Definitions (lines 21217–21268)**
- CIA: Carrier Incremental Availability (continuous depot maintenance/modernization, CVN 68 class)
- DMP: Depot Modernization Period (major high priority warfare improvement alterations)
- DPIA: Docking Planned Incremental Availability (less than 1 year, carriers in IMP)
- DPMA: Docking Phased Maintenance Availability
- DSRA: Docking Selected Restricted Availability (dry-dock required)
- EDPMA: Extended DPMA
- EDSRA: Extended DSRA
- ERP: Extended Refit Period
- EOH: Engineered Overhaul (nuclear submarines, reactor not refueled)
- ERO: Engineered Refueling Overhaul (nuclear submarines, reactor refueled)
- MMP: Major Maintenance Period (onsite non-CNO for SSGNs)
- OH: Overhaul (major, normally exceeding 6 months)
- PIA: Planned Incremental Availability (less than 6 months, carriers in IMP)
- PIRA: Pre-Inactivation Restricted Availability
- P-MA: Phased Maintenance Availability
- RA/TA: Restricted/Technical Availability (depot level support/maintenance)
- SCO: Service Craft Overhaul
- SRA: Selected Restricted Availability (short labor-intensive, ships in Progressive or EOC Maintenance Programs)

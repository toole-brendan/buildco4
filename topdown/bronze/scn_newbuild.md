All dollar figures in $thousands as they appear in P-1 TOA columns.

**Metric used: Total Obligation Authority (TOA)** — includes net P-1 procurement, subsequent full funding (SFF), advance procurement (AP), and completion of prior-year shipbuilding (CTC). This represents the total funding flowing to industry in each fiscal year.

**Note on FY26 Total:** FY26 figures include both the President's Budget Request and Reconciliation amounts. Many programs have significant funding in the Reconciliation column (e.g., DDG-51: $10,773K Request + $5,400,000K Reconcil = $5,410,773K Total). The Reconciliation amounts represent additional ships/funding beyond the discretionary request.
